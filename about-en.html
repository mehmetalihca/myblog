About Me

Hello! I'm Mehmet Ali, a passionate student of Computer Engineering and Industrial Engineering. My journey in these two disciplines has allowed me to combine technical problem-solving with strategic thinking, giving me a unique edge in both software development and systems optimization.

I was born in Hatay, and I have always been curious about how things work. That curiosity led me to engineering. At university, I discovered my love for building projects that are both functional and impactful—ranging from network programming tools to real-time web applications and data science experiments.

Besides tech, I love writing, traveling, and sharing what I learn. This blog is my digital corner to tell stories from my academic journey, the projects I’ve built, and my Erasmus experience across Europe.

Feel free to explore the rest of my site to learn more about the things I do and the places I've been.
