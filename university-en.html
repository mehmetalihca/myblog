My University Experience

Studying both Computer Engineering and Industrial Engineering has been a unique and challenging journey. Juggling double majors meant long nights, overlapping deadlines, and a steep learning curve — but it also shaped me into a versatile and resilient problem-solver.

I’ve spent countless hours in the library, participated in student communities, and worked on multidisciplinary projects. I also contributed to a university podcast where I shared my experience as a double major student.

This phase of my life taught me how to manage time, embrace challenges, and collaborate effectively. Every exam week was a new adventure, and every presentation a learning opportunity.
